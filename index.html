The Meme

Last Friday, a powerful video of giraffes standing calmly in the middle of a thunderstorm spread across X and other platforms. With nowhere to hide, the towering animals endured rain and lightning in complete stillness.

The symbolism resonated deeply: strength without panic. Exposure without retreat. Standing tall when the storm has nowhere to miss you.

The internet turned it into a metaphor for resilience, unity, and endurance — and that spirit became the foundation of $GIRAFFES.
